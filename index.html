Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the OpenGym application (hereby referred to as "the App"), developed by Gabriel Halaweh. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have complete control over your data.

1. Data Collection and Storage

OpenGym is designed to operate entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information, fitness data, or usage metrics on external servers. All data created, captured, or entered into the App remains strictly local to your device.

2. Device Permissions

To provide core functionality, OpenGym may request access to the following device features:

  • Camera and Photo Gallery (Limited): To allow you to take or upload photos within the App. These images are processed and stored solely on your local device storage.
  • Audio / Microphone: To enable local audio features required for the App's operation. No audio is recorded, transmitted, or stored off-device.

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device's settings, though doing so may limit certain features within the App.

3. Third-Party Services

The App respects your privacy and does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or payment processors. Your usage remains completely private.

4. Data Deletion

Because OpenGym does not store any of your data on external servers, you have complete control over your information. To permanently delete all data associated with the App, you simply need to clear the App's data in your device settings or uninstall OpenGym from your device.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. Since all data is stored locally on the user's device, we do not hold any data regarding children on our servers.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
